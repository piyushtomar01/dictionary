                             DICTIONARY
                           
A Dictionary is a mutable and unordered collections of items each item is the
pair of keys and values.

Syntax: var_name={key:value}

eg. dict={"apple":14,"mango":10,"banana":5} 

NOTE:
Keys are unique within a dictionary while values may not be.The values of a
dictionary can be of any type,but the keys must be of an immutable datatype 
such as strings,numbers or tuples.
